Why a custom build beats patching WordPress

  • Speed: Storm-season searches happen on phones with weak signal — every second counts.
  • Control: No plugin conflicts breaking counters, forms, or layout after updates.
  • Proof: Gallery, warranties, and team bios stay intentional — not theme defaults.
  • Cost: One build + modest monthly ops beats repeated emergency WP fixes.
